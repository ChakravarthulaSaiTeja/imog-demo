## How to Organize

1. **Drop all images** into `/public/images/imog/img/` folder
2. **Manually move/copy** images from `img/` to the appropriate folders above
3. **Rename** images to match the naming convention (e.g., `hero-01.png`, `hackers-01.png`)
4. **Supported formats:** `.png`, `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.webp`
